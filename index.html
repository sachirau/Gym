Personal Training

At APEX Fitness, we try and maintain a ratio of one fitness trainer to five members so that all the members get proper attention. This helps in getting motivated, maintaining and correcting posture, pushing the body to its limits along with the right breathing patterns.During the personal training sessions the trainer explains the do’s and dont’s of the workouts. He then designs the workout schedule as per the member’s specific requirements

Cardio Training

Cardio is the all-in-one package if you want to burn fat, lose weight, get in shape, or simply be fit. Cardio is one of the quintessential exercises offered in many gyming facilities.Cardio exercises increase the heart and respiratory rate and increase the blood and oxygen flow throughout the body. The main reason peoples do a cardio workout is to burn calories to help them lose weight.The Cardio exercise focuses not only on burning fat but also increases stamina.

Weight Training

A very important aspect of a good physique is toned muscles. Weight training aims to achieve exactly the same.Our sessions in weight training focus on increasing the strength and size of skeletal muscles. As a result, you get in shape and can flaunt those high-earned body muscles.Weight Training includes a set of well-designed exercises mainly to increase the strength and efficiency of muscles. For each muscle type, at APEX Fitness we use a set of specific machines and instruments.
