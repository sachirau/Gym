About APEX Fitness and Our Team

The Leading Gym in Pune

APEX Fitness is a Unisex Gym in Pune located at Dhankawadi, promoting fitness and wellness since August 1, 2016. At TranceForm Fitness, we offer a variety of packages designed to help you achieve your fitness objectives while adding excitement to your workouts by combining fitness with fun.

At our Club, you will find an excellent ambiance along with a team of certified trainers who are always by your side. We ensure that our members receive the best services, including scientifically designed equipment and workout schedules, helping them achieve their fitness goals while minimizing the risk of injuries.

Our main motto is to maintain a ratio of one trainer to five members so that everyone receives proper attention. This approach helps in staying motivated, maintaining and correcting posture, and pushing your body to its limits while practicing the right breathing patterns.

To add extra fun to your cardio workout, we offer a wide range of group activities, including Yoga, Power Yoga, Aerobics, Fusion, Bollywood Dance, Zumba, Cardio Kickboxing, and much more. Each batch is led by a dedicated expert in that particular field.

Additionally, we offer a one-day free trial at our club, during which you can explore our fully equipped weight training and cardio sections, along with our group activities.

We cordially invite you to become a part of the TranceForm Fitness family today.
